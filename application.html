How to play the game called love?

This game can be interacted with in different ways. The story mode gets you aquanted with a highschool girl named Grace and you sort of play her inner conciouse. Of course her journey to love is laid by many difficult choices and you are there to give her advice. Its a game that can go in many different ways. If you hate boys, then you might lead Grace to do the same. But if you believe in true love on a particular day, then Grace will be glad to explore that with you!

Another aspect of the game is the infinitly intriguing name tester. Here you can compare two names and see how compatible the super high tech love tester says they are!

Here Lisa and Adolph stand a mediocre chance of love, not too encouraging for the pair. But you can pair any name with any name. This can be fun for hours. Losing in this game is purely perspective. Even if you have a name such as Gleepglop there is going to be someone out there for ya with a better than ninety percent chance of success.
