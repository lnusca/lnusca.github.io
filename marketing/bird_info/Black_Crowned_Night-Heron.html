Black Crowned Night-Heron

The Night Heron lives almost everywhere in the world—Australia and Antarctica are the only places it doesn’t. A night hunter that can sit still for hours waiting for prey, the Night Heron is also a social bird, living in noisy colonies and proving to be an amazing parent to its chicks. Piercing red eyes give it an otherworldly stare, perfect for it’s shadowy, nocturnal world.
